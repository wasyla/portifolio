About

I am currently doing PhD in Education at the Queensland University of Technology (QUT). My research focusses on understanding how technology is used in teaching and learning in secondary schools. Right now I am at the final stage of the PhD journey. Prior to joining QUT, I worked as a researcher (deputy director) and lecturer at the Institute for Research and Innovation, Villa College, one of the prestigous institutions in the Maldives. I have 5+ years experience in the field of social science research, and more than 10 years experience as an academician for higher education institutions. I am really passionate about researching ways to engage students in learning through technology
